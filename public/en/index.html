About Us

We support

RAM e.V. supports your research financially (e.g. for your participation in conferences or financing of data sets and experiments)

We promote

RAM e.V. promotes your research our a variety of social media channels (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter)

We award

RAM e.V. annually honors outstanding theses in political science, psychology and sociology
